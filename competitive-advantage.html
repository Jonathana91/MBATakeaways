Competitive Advantage

TL;DR: If your edge disappears when you stop pushing, it is not a real edge.

Study Order Strategy #3 Competitive Advantage
Why it matters

You need something structural: cost advantage, switching costs, network effects, brand, distribution, or a regulatory moat.

How to use it
  • Name your edge in one sentence.
  • Ask whether a competitor can copy it in 6–12 months.
  • Prefer structural over temporary advantages.
Real-life example

Amazon’s logistics scale is not just “good execution.” It lowers costs and improves speed in a way smaller rivals struggle to match.

Where people screw it up

Confusing a feature, a campaign, or hustle with a moat.

Bottom line

If your edge disappears when you stop pushing, it is not a real edge.
